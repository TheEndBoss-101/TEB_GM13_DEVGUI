## Q & A / FAQ

>1:  
> Q: How do i download?  
> A: Click code and download as a .zip

>2:  
> Q: What addon folder?  
> A: C:\Program Files (x86)\Steam\steamapps\common\GarrysMod\garrysmod\addons

> Q: What do you mean by unzip?
